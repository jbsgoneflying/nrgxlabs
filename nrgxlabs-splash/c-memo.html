Abstract

NRGX Labs is a private research desk for self-directed capital. We publish calibrated, regime-aware trading research — built by traders, run as a lab, with every signal verified at runtime against a live shadow desk before it ships.

What follows is the short version of what we do, how we do it, and who it's for. The full work lives behind the door at app.nrgxlabs.com.

§1

What we research

Four areas where we have an edge and a public track. Each is its own engine inside the lab.

  1. i. Earnings IV crush. Selling earnings premium on names whose realized vol consistently undershoots the implied move.
  2. ii. SPX iron condors. Weekly and 0DTE wing placement, sized to the live regime rather than a static rulebook.
  3. iii. Regime intelligence. Learned regime embeddings that flag when the tape has changed beneath you, before the P&L tells you.
  4. iv. Cross-asset pairs. Funded, mean-reverting pairs across equities, rates, and vol — sized by drawdown, not by Sharpe.
§2

How we work

The methodology spine is short. Probabilities are conformal-calibrated, so a 70% prediction means 70% over the lookback. Features are regime-aware, so a signal that worked in 2019 vol doesn't get to vote in 2026 vol. Every change is runtime-verified against a live shadow desk before it reaches the operator. Research that fails replication doesn't ship.

"If we can't reproduce the edge in a forward window, we don't publish it. The desk is downstream of the proof."
§3

Who it's for

Self-directed traders running real capital. Single-LP funds. Family-office allocators who want the model behind the trade and not the marketing around it. If you want a glossy pitch, we are comfortably the wrong shop. If you want the residuals from the backtest, we are likely the right one.

Access

The lab is open by invitation. Have a code? Walk in. Don't? Every note to desk@nrgxlabs.com gets read by the desk, not a funnel.

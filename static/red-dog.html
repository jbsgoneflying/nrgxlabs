How to Trade Red Dog Reversals

Entry Rules
  • Bullish: Buy stop above reversal day's high
  • Bearish: Sell stop below reversal day's low
  • Confirm open is within prior day's range
Stop Rules
  • Bullish: Stop at Low B (reversal day low)
  • Bearish: Stop at High B (reversal day high)
  • Add buffer: ATR(14) × 0.25 or $0.10
Targets
  • T1: 1R (risk-reward 1:1)
  • T2: 2R (risk-reward 1:2)
  • T3: SMA20 mean reversion
Position Sizing
  • Scale position to 1-2% account risk
  • Take 50% off at T1
  • Trail remainder with prior bar stop
